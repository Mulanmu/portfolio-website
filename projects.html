CS220 Project
Our group presents a design of a social media application for elementary students. Our goal is to create a safe and fun online space for the students to connect with each other.
LostArt
LostArt is a group project I worked on when I was taking the Extending the museum course at MIT last semester. We coorporated with the MFA Boston and created a prototype for the museum and visitors to discover the less viewed and exposed artworks at the museum with help of Instagram data analysis.
Inside Vase
This project is based on the Greek collection from the Davis Museum at Wellesley. I created a 3D model and wireframe that intend to allow students to explore interpretations and create narratives from the collection. It is a virtual space that students can examine models of Greek vases, discuss with others, and write down their ideas.
